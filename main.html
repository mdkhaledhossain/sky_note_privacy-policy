Privacy Policy for SKY Note

1. Introduction

Welcome to SKY Note. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our extension.

We collect the following data:

3. Data Use

The data we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Storing your notes locally on your device.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties.

5. User Rights

You can manage your notes directly within the extension. You have the right to delete any note at any time.
